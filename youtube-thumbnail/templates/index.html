YouTube Thumbnail FAQs

How big is a YouTube thumbnail?

1280x720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio) is the best YouTube thumbnail size recommended by YouTube as it's the most used in YouTube players and previews. JPG, GIF, and PNG image formats are accepted, and the file size should be less than 2MB.

How to add a thumbnail to your YouTube video?

Follow the below tips to add thumbnails for your videos.

Add a custom thumbnail for published videos:

  1. Sign in to YouTube, click your profile picture and select YouTube Studio.
  2. Click “Videos” from the left menu, and select the video you’d love to add a custom thumbnail.
  3. Click “Upload thumbnail” under the “Details” tap to select a thumbnail image from your computer. You can also scroll down to choose an auto-generated thumbnail you like as your video thumbnail.

Add a custom thumbnail when uploading the video:

While uploading videos on YouTube, you will see three images under “Video Thumbnails”. They’re auto-generated screenshots from your video. You can set one of them as your video thumbnail, or upload a thumbnail image from your computer by clicking “Custom thumbnail”.
